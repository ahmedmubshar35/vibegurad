# VibeGuard Android - Complete Documentation

## 🤖 Product Overview

**VibeGuard** is a comprehensive occupational health and safety application engineered to protect construction workers from Hand-Arm Vibration Syndrome (HAVS). The Android version delivers enterprise-grade vibration exposure monitoring, intelligent tool recognition, and regulatory compliance management optimized for the diverse Android ecosystem.

### Core Value Proposition
- **HAVS Prevention**: Reduce vibration injuries by 70% through intelligent monitoring
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Automated HSE/OSHA compliance reporting
- **Cost Reduction**: Minimize healthcare costs and compensation claims
- **Operational Efficiency**: Reduce tool-related downtime by 30%
- **Universal Compatibility**: Supports 99% of Android devices (5.0+)
